Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kids Academy secure?

Yes! Kids Academy uses enterprise-grade security including JWT authentication, HTTPS encryption, role-based access control, and strict data isolation for multi-school support.

Can I manage multiple schools?

Absolutely! The Multi-School plan allows you to manage unlimited schools with complete data isolation. Each school has its own separate data, users, and administrators.

How do teachers get their login credentials?

Only administrators can create teacher accounts and set passwords. Teachers cannot create their own accounts or modify login credentials. Admins can reset passwords at any time.

Can I export reports to Excel?

Yes! You can export attendance reports, fee records, and salary information to Excel with a single click. Reports are automatically formatted and ready for analysis.

What about data backups?

We provide automatic daily backups and you can download backups anytime. Enterprise plans include on-premise backup options and custom retention policies.

Is there a mobile app?

The web application is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. Native mobile apps are coming in 2025 as part of our roadmap.
